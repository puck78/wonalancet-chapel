The Wonalancet Chapel is an ecumenical community that hosts visiting ministers and lay speakers during the summer months. They present sermons from their spiritual and ethical experience, whether they are lay or ordained.


This website is intended to host a small collection of digitized sermons, postcards from Wonalancet history, and links to various activities carried out by our community, including recorded sermons fro the summer of 2020
